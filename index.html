The Science

Stone Age minerals.
Modern formulation.

Our ancestors' diet delivered abundant potassium, magnesium, calcium and bicarbonate — the alkaline mineral salts that human biology evolved with over millions of years. Contemporary diets and RO-purified water have systematically reversed this. The result is a chronic, low-grade mineral deficit that accumulates silently with age.


Stonaje holds an exclusive technology license from CSIR-CSMCRI (Patent IN309175) for a patented natural mineral fortification process — the Dry Sea Mix technology. Solar-evaporated seawater fractions are blended to produce a full-spectrum mineral salt containing calcium, magnesium, potassium, sulphate and bicarbonate in naturally balanced ratios. Zero synthetic chemicals. Zero excess sodium.
